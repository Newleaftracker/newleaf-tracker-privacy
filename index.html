Privacy Policy for NewLeaf Tracker

NewLeaf Tracker is a Chrome Extension designed to help newcomers to Canada track their immigration journey. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. All data stays within your browser and is not transmitted to any server.

Permissions

NewLeaf Tracker may require permission to store data locally using Chrome's storage API. This is solely to save your progress and checklist items.

Data Security

All data is stored locally in your browser and is not accessible to any external source. No data is sent to the developers or third parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
