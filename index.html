Privacy Policy for NewLeaf Tracker

Effective Date: July 20, 2025

NewLeaf Tracker is a Chrome Extension designed to help newcomers to Canada track their immigration progress, including permanent residency (PR) steps, document checklists, and reminders. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) or sensitive user data to external servers. All data you input into NewLeaf Tracker is stored locally in your browser using Chrome’s storage.sync API.

  • Your data stays on your device and is only synced across your own Chrome browsers if logged into the same Google account.
  • We do not access, collect, or transmit this data.

2. No Third-Party Access

We do not share your information with third parties. NewLeaf Tracker does not use third-party analytics tools, trackers, or ad services.

3. Data Security

We rely on Chrome’s secure storage.sync mechanism. For your protection, we recommend:

  • Using strong passwords on your Google account
  • Enabling two-factor authentication

4. User Control

You can:

  • Reset or clear your checklist data from the extension
  • Uninstall the extension at any time to remove local data

5. Children’s Privacy

This extension is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy due to feature changes, the revised version will be posted here with a new effective date.
